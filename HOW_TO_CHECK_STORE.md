## Next Steps

### 1. **Add Product Images** (Manual)
- Go to: `/admin/products`
- Click on each product
- Click "Add image" ‚Üí Upload high-quality product photos
- Images significantly boost sales!

### 2. **Customize Store Theme**
- Go to: `/admin/themes`
- Choose a professional theme
- Customize colors, fonts, layout

### 3. **Set Up Payment Methods**
- Go to: `/admin/settings/payments`
- Enable Shopify Payments or PayPal
- Test checkout process

### 4. **Set Up Shipping**
- Go to: `/admin/settings/shipping`
- Configure shipping rates
- Set up free shipping threshold ($50)

### 5. **Store Policies**
- Go to: `/admin/settings/legal`
- Add Privacy Policy, Refund Policy, Terms of Service
- Templates provided in improvement script
