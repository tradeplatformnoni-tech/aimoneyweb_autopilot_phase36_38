**Service:** cheeee-webhook-trade

## Step-by-Step Instructions

### Step 1: Open Render Dashboard
✅ You already have it open: https://dashboard.render.com

### Step 2: Navigate to Service
1. In the left sidebar, click on **"cheeee-webhook-trade"**
2. You should see the service details page

### Step 3: Go to Settings
1. In the left sidebar, click **"Settings"**
2. Scroll down to find the **"Danger Zone"** section

### Step 4: Suspend Service
1. Look for **"Suspend Service"** button
2. Click **"Suspend Service"**
3. Confirm the suspension when prompted

### Step 5: Verify
- Service status should change to "Suspended"
- Service will stop running
- Data and configuration are preserved

## What Happens When You Suspend

✅ **Service stops running** - No more resource usage  
✅ **Data preserved** - All environment variables, configs saved  
✅ **Can reactivate** - Can resume later if needed  
✅ **Can delete later** - Can still delete after suspending  
✅ **No cost** - Free tier, but stops any potential activity

## After Suspending

The service will:
- Stop running

- Can be resumed or deleted later
