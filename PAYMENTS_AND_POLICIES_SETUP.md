## ğŸ’³ Payment Methods Setup

### Option 1: Shopify Payments (Recommended)

**Why Shopify Payments?**
- No transaction fees (just credit card processing fees)
- Faster payouts (same day or next day)
- Built into Shopify
- Accepts major credit cards

**Setup Steps:**
1. Go to: https://trendytreasure-market.myshopify.com/admin/settings/payments
2. Find **"Shopify Payments"** section
3. Click **"Complete account setup"** or **"Activate Shopify Payments"**
4. Fill in business information:
   - Business type (Sole proprietorship, LLC, etc.)
   - Business address
   - Tax ID (SSN or EIN)
   - Bank account details (routing and account number)
5. Submit for verification (usually takes 1-3 business days)
6. Once approved, Shopify Payments will be automatically enabled

**Required Documents:**
- Government-issued ID (Driver's license or Passport)
- Proof of address (Utility bill or bank statement)
- Bank account statement (for verification)

---

### Option 2: PayPal

**Why PayPal?**
- Quick setup (can be activated immediately)
- Customers trust PayPal
- No monthly fees
- Good for international customers

**Setup Steps:**
1. Go to: https://trendytreasure-market.myshopify.com/admin/settings/payments
2. Click **"Add payment provider"**
3. Select **"PayPal"**
4. Choose **"PayPal Express Checkout"**
5. Click **"Connect PayPal account"**
6. Log in to your PayPal business account
7. Authorize Shopify to access PayPal
8. Enable **"PayPal Express Checkout"**
9. Save settings

**Note:** You can enable BOTH Shopify Payments and PayPal to give customers more payment options!

## ğŸ“„ Store Policies Setup

### Why Policies Are Important
- Builds customer trust
- Required by law in many regions
- Protects your business
- Reduces disputes and returns

### Setup Steps

1. **Go to Legal Settings:**
   ```
   https://trendytreasure-market.myshopify.com/admin/settings/legal
   ```

2. **Add Privacy Policy:**
   - Click **"Create from template"** or **"Add policy"**
   - Select **"Privacy Policy"**
   - Copy content from `policy_privacy_policy.html` (created by script)
   - Paste into the editor
   - Click **"Save"**

3. **Add Refund Policy:**
   - Click **"Add policy"**
   - Select **"Refund Policy"**
   - Copy content from `policy_refund_policy.html`
   - Paste and save

4. **Add Terms of Service:**
   - Click **"Add policy"**
   - Select **"Terms of Service"**
   - Copy content from `policy_terms_of_service.html`
   - Paste and save
