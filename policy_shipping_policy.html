Shipping Policy

Last Updated: November 2025

Shipping Information

At TrendyTreasure Market, we want to get your products to you as quickly and safely as possible. All orders are processed within 1-2 business days (excluding weekends and holidays) after your payment is confirmed.

Shipping Rates and Delivery Times

  • Standard Shipping (Free on orders over $50): 7-14 business days - $4.99
  • Express Shipping: 5-10 business days - $9.99
  • Priority Shipping: 3-7 business days - $14.99

Note: Delivery times are estimates and may vary depending on your location and carrier service.

Free Shipping

Enjoy FREE standard shipping on all orders over $50! Simply use code FREESHIP50 at checkout (if not automatically applied).

International Shipping

We currently ship to the following countries:

  • United States (including Alaska and Hawaii)
  • Canada
  • United Kingdom
  • Australia
  • Most European Union countries

International shipping rates: Calculated at checkout based on destination and weight.

International delivery: 10-30 business days depending on destination and customs processing.

Processing Time

All orders are processed Monday through Friday (excluding holidays). Orders placed after 2:00 PM EST will be processed the next business day.

  • In-Stock Items: Shipped within 1-2 business days
  • Pre-Order Items: Clearly marked with estimated shipping dates

Order Tracking

Once your order ships, you'll receive:

  • Shipping confirmation email with tracking number
  • Updates at each stage of delivery
  • Estimated delivery date

Track your order using the link provided in your shipping confirmation email.

Shipping Address

Please ensure your shipping address is correct at checkout. We are not responsible for orders shipped to incorrect addresses provided by the customer. If you need to change your address after ordering, contact us immediately at tradeplatformnoni@gmail.com.

Customs and Duties

International Orders: Customers are responsible for any customs duties, taxes, or fees charged by their country. These fees are not included in the product or shipping price. We cannot estimate these charges, as they vary by country and are determined by local customs authorities.

Lost or Stolen Packages

If your package is lost or stolen:

  1. Check with your local post office or carrier
  2. Contact us immediately at tradeplatformnoni@gmail.com with your order number
  3. We will investigate and work with the carrier to resolve the issue
  4. If confirmed lost, we will ship a replacement at no cost

Damaged Packages

If you receive a damaged package:

  • Take photos of the damaged package and product
  • Contact us within 48 hours of delivery
  • We will send a replacement immediately

Undeliverable Packages

If a package is returned to us due to an incorrect address, refusal of delivery, or failure to pick up from the post office:

  • We will contact you to update the address
  • Additional shipping fees may apply for reshipment
  • If unclaimed after 30 days, a refund will be issued minus shipping costs

Multiple Items

If your order contains multiple items, they may ship in separate packages. You will receive tracking information for each package. No additional shipping charges apply.

Holiday Shipping

During peak holiday seasons (November-December), shipping times may be extended. We recommend ordering early to ensure delivery by your desired date. Updated shipping deadlines will be posted on our homepage during holiday seasons.

PO Boxes and Military Addresses

We ship to PO Boxes and APO/FPO addresses using standard shipping methods. Express shipping options may not be available for these addresses.
